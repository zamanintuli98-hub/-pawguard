Questions
Everything you need to know.
What is PawGuard exactly?
+

PawGuard is an interactive dog health companion — a web app you access from any device (phone, tablet, desktop) plus a downloadable 40-page PDF guide. It includes a daily health dashboard, meal tracker, symptom checker, personalised health quiz and 24/7 AI support from PawBot. No app download is required — it works in your browser.

How do I access it after buying?
+

After purchase on Whop, you'll get instant access to the PawGuard web app and a download link for the PDF guide — all inside your Whop account. You can also download the iOS or Android app once it's live on the stores (coming very soon).

Is PawBot replacing a vet?
+

No — PawBot is a knowledgeable AI companion, not a licensed veterinarian. It helps you understand what's happening and when to seek professional care. Think of it as a brilliant dog-loving friend who happens to know a lot about dog health. Always consult a vet for anything that persists or concerns you.

Does it work for all dog breeds?
+

Yes! When you set up your dog's profile you select their breed, and PawGuard customises nutrition goals, exercise targets and health benchmarks accordingly. The PDF guide includes breed-specific data for 10 of the most popular breeds, and the app works for any breed or mix.

How do I cancel my subscription?
+

Cancel anytime from your Whop account settings — no phone calls, no email requests, no waiting. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. The Lifetime plan has no recurring charge.

Is there a money-back guarantee?
+

Yes — every plan comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If PawGuard isn't right for you within 30 days of purchase, contact us and we'll refund you in full. No questions asked.
